BUILD A BOT

The Build a Bot event at D3 Fest is a thrilling challenge that invites tech enthusiasts and budding engineers to unleash their creativity and technical skills. Participants are tasked with designing, building, and showcasing their own robots in a dynamic and competitive environment.

Key Highlights: Innovative Robot Design: Participants create functional robots tailored to specific tasks or challenges. Hands-On Learning: Explore the principles of robotics, including mechanics, sensors, and programming. Collaboration & Competition: Work in teams or individually to push the boundaries of innovation and compete with peers. Showcase Your Talent: Demonstrate your skills to an audience of tech experts and fellow enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced builder, Build a Bot is your chance to experiment, innovate, and bring your robotic vision to life at D3 Fest!

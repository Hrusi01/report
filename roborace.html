ROBO RACE

The Robo Race event at D3 Fest is an adrenaline-pumping challenge designed to test speed, precision, and technical ingenuity. This thrilling competition brings together robotics enthusiasts from across the country to design, build, and race their robots through a demanding obstacle course.

Speed and Precision: Participants must construct robots capable of navigating a track filled with twists, turns, and hurdles, all while competing against the clock.

Innovative Engineering: Showcase your creativity and technical prowess by designing robots that are agile, efficient, and robust enough to overcome challenges.

Competitive Spirit: Teams battle head-to-head, pushing their robots to the limit, creating an electrifying atmosphere of competition and camaraderie.

Learning Opportunity: Engage with cutting-edge robotics concepts such as motion control, sensors, and optimization, and gain practical experience in real-world problem-solving.

Why Participate?
Unleash your engineering skills and tackle an exciting robotics challenge.
Compete with like-minded innovators and tech enthusiasts.
Experience the thrill of robotics in action and win recognition and prizes.
Robo Race is more than a competition—it’s a showcase of talent, determination, and technological brilliance. Gear up to design, race, and conquer at D3 Fest, IIIT Bhubaneswar!
